My "Delectable" Setup

I'm broke and that reflects on my "amazing super powerful" computer and peripherals. I'm the "proud" owner of shitbox PC with an old ASUS Mobo, a shitty AM4 AMD APU, a 1TB HDD and no GPU. I make all my digital art with my Wacom Intuos Pen Small CTL-480 and GIMP(GNU Image Manipulation Program). My most used programs are: GIMP(drawing and image manipulation), Libresprite(pixel art), Reaper(music production), Audacity(sfx work) and GameMaker(making games duhh).


I guess I'm kinda living proof you don't need good gear to make good stuff lol.
